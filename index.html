About us

Get to know us a little bit!

John L. Doe

John is a calm, collected and introverted young man. He is deeply passionate about the environment and is an active advocate for green energy. He is an artist at a local organisation. John loves mountain climbing, playing his guitar and camping. In addition, he is a renowned artist and some of his work adorns the largest art museum in Harare! John teaches art to the children at his church every Sunday.

Ruth R. Mazango

Ruth is a bubbly, outgoing, funny, spiritual, adventurous and remarkable young woman. She loves the outdoors and her hobbies include hiking, camping, partying and reading. She occasionally volunteers at her local community and organises monthly clothes drive for donations to the orphanage at her church. She is successful web developer and owns her own business.
